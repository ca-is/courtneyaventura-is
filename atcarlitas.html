Carlita’s

Tacos, Tequila, and Whiskey

As an entirely new concept, I was able to build the brand from the ground up. When they said, “Think Desperado, Wild West,” I seized the chance to try my hand at some custom lettering.

In building out more of the brand story, the rhymes came easily, crafting a story about this fictional Carlita heroine — “the best in the land, the most courageous of heart and quickest of hand.”

Brand Story

Art Direction, Design, Illustration, Copywriting

In need of a secondary logo element and wanting to develop more of a story for the new brand, I couldnt help the rhymes that came to mind.

It started first with Carlitas: Where the margaritas make everyone bonitas and was followed quickly by...

Seoritas prefer Carlitas.

Muchachos like our tacos.

Carlita once knew a bad hombre or two. She would pull out her pistol and spin it round, and before yer knew it, he was flat on the ground.

All those seores knew she was mejores the best in the land, the most courageous of heart and quickest of hand.
